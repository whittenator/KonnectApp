Frequently Asked Questions 


1. What is Konnect? Konnect is the ultimate nightlife app where you can find the best specials, hottest events, and newest spots for your perfect night out. 


2. How do I setup my profile? You can edit your profile by tapping on the “Edit” icon at the top right of your profile.


 3. I’m a user and an owner -- how do I access my accounts? To access another account, simply tap the hamburger menu on the top left corner of your screen, and choose the Log Out option. Once logged out, you’ll have the ability to log back in with any account. 


4. I can’t post -- what’s up? Users can post by checking into bars or events. Once there, the post will appear on the bar or event page and the users own page. If a bar is not registered with Konnect, you cannot post on their page. If you are still having issues posting to Konnect pages, please contact info@thekonnectapp.com


 5. How do I claim my venue? Claiming your venue is easy. Just search your bar or restaurant’s name, or click on your map icon. We’ll send a verification code to your email after you’ve added some basic information, and you’ll be able to post, add specials, and create events in no time.
